Frontend Enablement üí™

What?

Part of the Engineering Enablement team, Frontend Enablement focuses on supplying the tools necessary to solve domain agnostic front-end concerns.

How?

Frontend Enablement believes in each front-end team to have complete ownership of their individual experience-driven ecosystems, promoting the success of this model by simplifying the communication between design and frontend teams and harvesting domain agnostic shared strategies into common UI tools.
